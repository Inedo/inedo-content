1.3 is released!

Note: This is not the latest version of Otter.

Otter maintains your servers' configuration using an Infrastructure as Code approach, and with Otter 1.3, you now have the access to all the Inedo integration through the Inedo Den. Allowing you to link in with all your DevOps tools.

If you're upgrading, make sure to check out Upgrade Notes for Otter 1.3.

Extension and Community portal

Adding even more extensibility, you can now not only hook into BuildMaster with infrastructure configuration sync but connect with the rest of your DevOps toolchain with extension both supported by Inedo and written by the community, through the Inedo Den.

Submit your Extensions

Have you created your own custom extension using the Otter SDK? Think it's useful for other folks? You can now submit your own extension to the community portal. Become an active member of the inedo community, and help others by sharing your content today!

Get started!

You can download Otter and take your own test drive, and see for yourself just how easy visualizing your infrastructure can be. If you have a feature you'd like to add, don't hesitate to contact us via the support Q&A forum.
