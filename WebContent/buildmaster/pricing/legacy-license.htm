Legacy License

Before v6.0, BuildMaster Free included 5 users, 10 applications, and 5 servers. Beginning with v6.0, BuildMaster Free is now unlimited users, applications, and servers, but all users act as full administrators (i.e. all users have every permission granted to them and restrictions are ignored).

Important: any Free Edition users that expose their instance to the internet and assign privileges to Anonymous users should be aware those users would become full administrators after upgrading to v6.0

If you are an existing free user who is negatively affected by this change, fill out the contact form below and we can send you a new key that will effectively be a 5 user key with no server or application restrictions.

Note: you must use the email associated with your account. If you're not sure of the correct email, include your current license key in the "additional comments" section.
