Also Available: Open Source Tools

UPack

UPack is a technology-neutral packaging platform that allows you to uniformly distribute your applications and components across environments to enable consistent deployment and testing.

Romp

Romp lets development and operations teams work closely together. Developers can specify exactly what infrastructure configuration their application requires. Which then allows operations to easily inspect the packages, and deploy them in a simple but controlled manner.
