Licensing Terms

ProGet is licensed annually per the ProGet End-User Licensing Agreement on a per-server basis; this license includes support and maintenance.

Perpetual Licensing Available

ProGet may also be licensed on a perpetual, non-expiring basis. Support, upgrades, and maintenance are included for the first year, and may be renewed each following year for 20% of the then-current licensing fees. You may pre-purchase up to three years of support.

The perpetual licensing fees are: $24,995 for Enterprise and $4,995 for Basic. Assuming support is renewed, the break-even point of Annual vs. Perpetual licensing comes after four years.
