Release Coordination with Calendars

Calendars in BuildMaster help you avoid communication bottlenecks, eliminate poorly timed deployments that monopolize server resources, and bring clarity to your application release process.

Including release coordination as part of your development process allows for achieving business goals faster. Poorly timed deployments often lead to delays in application releases, creates more overhead for QA and Testing, and can even cause server downtime; you can avoid all of these issues by using Calendars to plan your releases and ensure progress stays on track.

Environment Modeling with Otter

Otter lets you manage and configure the infrastructure where your applications are deployed; whether your servers are local, virtual, or cloud-built, you can define and create the exact environment required for your applications. With drift detection, Otter ensures that your servers are in the defined and desired configuration. With the option to switch between the text and visual editor, Otter makes its super simple to scale your environment as required by your application, microservices or containers.
