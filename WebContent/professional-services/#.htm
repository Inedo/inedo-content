Service Offerings

We offer three broad categories of Professional Services:

Assessment & Planning

This offering helps teams who are getting started with DevOps. This process is done in three stages:

  • Technical - The service team will review your application portfolio to see how the applications can be automated and migrated towards a DevOps process
  • Skills - This involves interviewing and testing your team(s) to learn their existing skills set and capabilities
  • Plan - We will devise a plan, specific to your organization, on how to improve the technology and increase the skills in an incremental manner for you to achieve DevOps success

Leadership & Team Coaching

This offering is designed for working with management and team leads to be the motivator for DevOps success. It specifically focuses on:

  • Establishing autonomous teams
  • Creating a DevOps-centric culture
  • Building an effective DevOps mindset

Resource Augmentation

Focus on your core responsibilities while we provide the engineering expertise to allow your enterprise to achieve the benefits of application automation and DevOps success sooner.
