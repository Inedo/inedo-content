Job Description

We are seeking a fun and outgoing person with exceptional people skills, and organizational abilities to represent Inedo at events across the country. This person will work with marketing professionals and vendors to ensure event success. 

This position will be responsible for attending trade shows and events to meet with our current and future users. This position requires a high level of people skills and willingness to engage with new people all the time.

Responsibilities/Duties

  • Coordinate scheduling and logistics for all trade shows, conferences, meetings and events
  • Travel to and support onsite execution and management of key events, including show set-up and take-down
  • Develop and maintain relationships with outside vendors
  • Manage shipping and receiving logistics
  • Managing inventory and tracking of marketing materials.
  • Reconcile event budgets
  • Ensure processing of show leads in CRM system
  • Support digital, direct, social and other media campaigns
  • Research and recommend marketing initiatives as needed
  • Train conference staff as needed

Qualifications

  • Willing to travel up to 50% to events as needed, may include travel on some evenings and weekends
  • Ability to pass background and Credit Check
  • Ability to stand for 8+ hours a day
  • Ability to lift 50lbs
  • Bachelor’s degree preferred
  • 3+ year professional experience (marketing or event planning preferred)
  • Ability to work closely in a team environment
  • Strong Interpersonal skills
  • Fluent verbal and written communication in English.
  • Proficient in MS Office
  • Proactive, hands-on, and good problem solving skills.
  • Previous trade show event experience preferred.
