Software Development Manager

Full Time • Negotiable Salary • Paid Relocation

We (Inedo) are a software product company that makes several popular automation tools that help organizations implement DevOps practices. Although we’re a small company, our products power the software delivery of some of the largest enterprises in the world, as well as countless other retail, government, medical, and financial institutions.

With the DevOps market exploding in the past couple years, we’ve seen a lot of growth as well, and are looking for a strong team player in our Berea, Ohio offices to lead our development team.

About Us

If you join our team, you'll find yourself working with passionate and driven professionals. We work hard, but more importantly, we work smart. This means not only eschewing buzzword-driven development and hacking-till-it-works, but we really focus on making a smart work/life balance a big part of our culture.

Responsibilities

The software development manager will be responsible for:

  • Manage software developers — work closely with software developers via daily stand-ups, task breakdown and assignment, training, hiring, and monitoring status of day-to-day development
  • Implement policy directives — define and enforce policies and processes, and ensure developer success through implementation of those policies, additionally providing feedback to upper management
  • Customer interaction — act as liaison between support staff, developers, and customers within a software development context

Experience

Experience Required

  • 5+ years application development experience (C#, .NET, Java, etc.)
  • 3+ years of management/leadership experience
  • 3+ years DevOps / automation exposure, including:
    • Continuous integration
    • Deployment automation

Preferred Experience

  • Knowledge of package management solutions (NuGet, maven, rpm, etc.)
  • Familiarity with Inedo tools
  • Experience with infrastructure automation (Puppet, Chef, Ansible)

Requirements

  • Expertise in application, data, and infrastructure architecture disciplines
  • Experience with leading projects through all phases of a software development lifecycle
  • Passion for learning, problem solving, and continuous improvement
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Effective interpersonal skills
  • Strong technical background, ideally in .NET development
  • Proficiency with Git version control (GitLab platform)
  • Familiarity with YouTrack platform for issue tracking management
