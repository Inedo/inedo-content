Responsibilities

The primary responsibility of this position is to offer technical advice to organizations who are trying to transform their organization through DevOps and automation. This is accomplished several ways, including:

  • Assess the technical needs and implementation challenges for DevOps and automation
  • Working directly with users of our software (primarily remotely) to develop a plan for a successful implementation of DevOps with Inedo products, and then regularly following up with them on a regular basis to drive this success
  • Demonstrating how Inedo products can help our users (primarily remotely) to users so they can better understand how the software might facilitate their use-case
  • Create written technical content to supplement these solutions, including blogs and tutorials
  • Present technical content to users remotely, either privately or publicly (in webinar)
