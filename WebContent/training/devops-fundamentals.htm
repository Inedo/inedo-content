DevOps Fundamentals

DevOps fundamentals provides a comprehensive introduction to the core DevOps principles and covers the essential knowledge and skill competences that have been defined by the DevOps Agile Skills Association (DASA), for which Inedo is a founding member.

Fundamentals course

This course outlines core DevOps terminology to aid in your understanding of DevOps principles and best practices. Through hands-on activities, group discussion, and real-word case studies, attendees will acquire a fundamental, comprehensive, understanding of DevOps.

Following the completion of this DASA DevOps Fundamentals course, you will be able to:

  • Explain the drivers responsible for the emergence of DevOps.
  • Define and discuss the key concepts and principles of DevOps.
  • List and explain the business benefits of DevOps and continuous delivery.
  • Describe the Service Delivery process.
  • Explain the concepts of test automation, infrastructure automation, and build and deployment automation.
  • Describe how DevOps relates to Lean and Agile methodologies.
  • Summarize case studies of IT organizations that are making the transformation to Adaptive IT and DevOps models.
  • List the most common and popular DevOps tools.
  • Discuss the critical success factors for DevOps implementation of DevOps behavior, mind set and culture, when transforming their organizations to the new world of IT.

This course is offered virtually, onsite, and licensed.

Seminar and Workshop

The seminar and workshop will provide a summary of the DevOps Fundamentals course but is intended for leaders and executives. This seminar will provide an understanding of why DevOps is important, will provide tangible steps on how to effectively prepare for a DevOps transformation, and will give you the support to lead change at your organization.

This DevOps Seminar and Workshop is offered both virtually and onsite.
