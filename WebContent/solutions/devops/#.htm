DevOps: For the Enterprise, not just Unicorns

The assumptions surrounding DevOps can sometimes be misleading, understandable so, as the term was initially popularized by companies like Etsy, Netflix, and Facebook: DevOps Unicorns. However, simply because your company doesn't fit into this category, doesn't mean you can't benefit from DevOps. In fact, DevOps Expert Gene Kim advocates that, "The majority of value for DevOps will be created in large, complex organizations".
