Accelerate & Scale Releases with Universal Packaging

  • Date:  May 10th, 2018
  • Presenter:  Alex Papadimoulis

Universal Packages, and Package Oriented-Development have become a standard in the DevOps community. If it's something you're moving to, considering, or just want to learn more about, you should take a look at the video, and at Hedgehog.

We created Hedgehog because of the frustrations that many of our users were encountering as they shifted to using packages for their own applications and components:

  • BuildMaster and other Application Release Automation tools are too "release" focused, not centered around packages
  • Other package-based tools get complicated at scale, and too few tool experts
    create a bottleneck

Hedgehog is not only intuitive and easy to use, but has a powerful, free version that has no limit on the number of projects, users, or servers!

In this webinar, Alex Papadimoulis gave an overview of Hedgehog, and demonstrated how you can use Hedgehog to accelerate and scale your releases.
