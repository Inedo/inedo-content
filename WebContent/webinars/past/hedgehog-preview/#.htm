A preview of our newest product, Hedgehog.

  • October 24, 2017
  • Presenter:  Alex Papadimoulis
  • Note: pre-release software

Hedgehog is designed to provide a centralized, web-based dashboard, for deploying Romp packages, and other release packages. This combines the usability and simplicity of Romp, with added release management features like pipelines and server targeting.

In this webinar, Inedo Founder and CEO, Alex Papadimoulis, gave a demonstration of our upcoming product, Hedgehog, and discussed Inedo's product direction and future vision.

Who's this webinar for?

Individuals who use, create, or are interested in universal packages, and romp packages, and those who want to learn more about our newest product, and our future product direction.

Webinar Outline:

  • Discussion of Inedo's Product Direction and Future Vision
  • Introduction to Packages at the Center of DevOps
  • Introduction to Hedgehog
  • Hedgehog Demo
  • Introduction to Hedgehog Pipelines, Releases, and Projects
