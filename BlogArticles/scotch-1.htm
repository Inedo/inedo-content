Scotch of the Month: Glenfiddich 12

As the Inedo "house" scotch, it was a unanimous decision to feature Glenfiddich 12 as the first Scotch of the Month.

Glenfiddich 12 was the scotch that started it all for us. When our CEO & Founder Alex Papadimoulis brought it into the office one day to enjoy a drink or two, we didn't realize how influential its presence would be. We now have over 10 different Glenfiddich bottles in our scotch library, and we are fond of all of them.

Sipping on the classic Glenfiddich 12 is always a great way for us to wind down after a long day of answering support emails and fixing bugs writing software.

Recently it helped rally company morale when our air conditioner decided to crap out on us for about a week. As much as we love sitting in the summer Cleveland heat dreading the upcoming winter, it wasn't exactly the ideal office situation... but we definitely made the best of it! A cigar and the '12 paired quite nicely on those afternoons.

We've also used it to inspire critical brainstorming sessions for our upcoming new office space. Stay tuned as we will be writing all about the new space in an upcoming post!
