All Day DevOps 2017 Viewing Party

Join Inedo and other local DevOps enthusiasts for a viewing party of Sonatype's All Day DevOps online event at the Inedo’s Cleveland headquarters on Tuesday, October 24 from 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM.

Pre-registration is required.

All Day DevOps is a free 24-hour live event, and includes 5 simultaneous tracks, 100 sessions, and is available in all time zones.

You heard us, 24 Hours. 100 Sessions. Live Online. Free!

Tracks include CI/CD, Modern Infrastructure and Monitoring, Automated Security, Cultural Transformations, DevOps in Government, and the newly created "Tech Crawl" where companies will invite you to a sneak peek of what it's like to work on DevOps in their organization.

We’ll serve snacks, drinks, and maybe even some scotch while we watch multiple sessions. We’re especially excited for these talks: Securing Modern Applications, Including The Database In A DevOps Process, Lessons From Our Journey To Infrastructure As Code, and many more!

Be sure to register for free ASAP as seats are limited.

For more information about the event go to http://www.alldaydevops.com/

We hope you can join us for the Cleveland area viewing party!
