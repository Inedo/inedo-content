Inedo again recognized in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Application Release Automation

Inedo has been recognized by Gartner, Inc. in the 2017 Magic Quadrant for Application Release Automation for its ability to execute and completeness of vision, and is listed in the Critical Capabilities for Application Release Automation Solutions report. This is the second consecutive year Inedo was included in these reports.

According to Gartner's report, "ARA tools enable best practices in moving application-related artifacts, applications, configurations and even data together across the application life cycle process. These tools are a key part of enabling the DevOps goal of achieving continuous delivery with large numbers of rapid, small releases."*

Gartner subscribers can view the entire Magic Quadrant report here.

"We appreciate this consecutive recognition in the Magic Quadrant from Gartner, the leading analysts in the technology industry. We think this recognition, combined with our successful and growing customer adoptions in 2017, are true testaments to the value our solution provides Enterprise level organizations," says Inedo President, and CEO, Alex Papadimoulis. "DevOps is more than just implementing new tools, it's about cultural transformation. We're proud that our Application Release Automation solution not only creates faster release cycles for customers, but also breaks down communication barriers, providing visibility across the entire organization's application life cycle."

As an Enterprise DevOps solution vendor, Inedo provides tools for not only Release Automation, but also Package Management, and Infrastructure Configuration Management. This tool suite provides a holistic approach to Enterprise DevOps. Inedo's solution allows organizations with stringent regulatory requirements to deliver ideas into production faster without sacrificing compliance. In addition, Inedo offers comprehensive training and services for Enterprises to transform organizational culture by removing communication barriers and building the foundation for success.
