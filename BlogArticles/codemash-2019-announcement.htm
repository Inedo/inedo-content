Come Visit Inedo At CodeMash

Inedo is kicking off 2019 by having our conference team attend CodeMash in Sandusky, Ohio!

CodeMash is a conference that generates approximately 2,500 attendees and focuses on educating developers on current practices, methodologies, and technology trends in a variety of platforms.

Stop by the Inedo booth (B6), or follow Inedo on Twitter for the latest conference updates. Our team will be ready to answer any of your questions about our products and chat about DevOps. We will also be participating in the KidzMash scavenger hunt, so make sure to come visit us to get the answer to our clue!

We look forward to seeing you at CodeMash!
