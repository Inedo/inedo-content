Launching DASA: DevOps Agile Skills Association

We’re happy to announce, in conjunction with other industry leaders, our involvement in the establishment of the DevOps Agile Skills Association (DASA), as one of the founding forerunners.

DASA, established with the objective of building a Universal Qualification Program for DevOps, is a non-profit organization that provides an open, global community for DevOps and Agile skills development.

We’re excited to be a part of this initiative and fuel the transition to DevOps by helping define DevOps roles and the learning objectives for those roles.
