Deployment Automation vs. Infrastructure as Code

Karl wrote an intriguing article breaking down the specifics of comparing Deployment Automation tools to Infrastructure as Code tools.

He bridges the gap with a clever metaphor involving Air Jordans vs. Tesla. He goes on to explain how a side-by-side comparison of the two doesn't quite cover what you need to know; even still, there's nothing wrong with having, and using, both tools.

He also delves into why Infrastructure as Code tools are becoming a DevOps preference for application package deployment, and also gives us a valuable reminder that there is no one-size-fits-all DevOps tool. Knowing your organizational needs first is the key to choosing the right tools, and successfully implementing DevOps best practices.
